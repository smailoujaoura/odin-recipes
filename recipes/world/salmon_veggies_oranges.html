Salmon and Veggies Recipe

This healthy and easy salmon dish is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner! It's packed with flavor and loaded with nutrients.

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets (about 6 oz each)
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or foil.
  2. Place the salmon fillets in the center of the baking sheet, skin-side down.
  3. In a bowl, toss the zucchini, bell pepper, and onion slices with 1 tbsp olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread the veggies around the salmon on the baking sheet.
  5. Drizzle the remaining 1 tbsp of olive oil over the salmon fillets. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Place lemon slices on top of the salmon and veggies.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley, if desired, and serve immediately.

Enjoy your healthy meal!
