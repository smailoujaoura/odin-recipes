Banana Bread Recipe

This classic banana bread recipe is perfect for using up overripe bananas. It's simple, moist, and delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup sugar (can reduce to 3/4 cup)
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C), and grease a 4x8-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth. Stir in the melted butter.
  3. Mix in the baking soda and salt. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
  4. Add the flour and stir until the batter is just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan.
  6. Bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Remove from the oven and let cool in the pan for a few minutes. Then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Slice and enjoy!

Happy Baking!
